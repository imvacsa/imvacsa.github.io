The Silent Struggle After Treatment

Survival is a victory, but it marks the beginning of a new journey. Ovarian cancer survivors face a complex web of physical and psychological challenges that require continuous, personalized support.

Pervasive Fatigue

A significant percentage of survivors report persistent fatigue that impacts their daily lives, making simple tasks feel monumental.

Chronic Pain

Ongoing pain and discomfort are common, requiring careful management to maintain quality of life.

Mental Health

Anxiety about recurrence and the stress of managing health can take a heavy toll on emotional well-being.

The Power of AI: From Reactive to Predictive

Artificial intelligence is the engine of this new model. By learning from each survivor's unique data, it can anticipate needs and help prevent crises before they occur, transforming care from reactive to predictive.
